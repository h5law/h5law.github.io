# Who even am I?

I have struggled with this question a lot. I have been many people, assumed
many faces. Some good, others bad, but none really me. I am not too old, -
yet not overly young either. I have lived an untraditional life that's for sure.
Battled addictions, dealt drugs, wrestled with my mental health, served prison
time, and time in mental hospitals. I've lived on the streets and jetskied along
the coasts of Dubai. In the midst of the utter lows and turbulent highs of my
manic existence, somehow I found god, disconnected from the real world and given
all I had away, I remained. Or more-so to how it felt, was finally sought out
earnestly for a change.

A few years on I am now writing this introductory post to my blog or thought
dump website. I aim to occasionally post updates here for my own sake. Document
ongoing projects or general periods of serious thought for some form of
preservation - whether deserved or not.

It was while in prison that my life drastically changed, not only did I find God
but I also found, more accurately I re-discovered, my love of learning, building
and turning pure thought into creative acts of their own. I learnt to live with,
some semblance of normality, adopting routine and structure into my chaotic life.
Who would've thought that with a new-found determination, drive and passion for
computing, technology, Philosophy and Theology, with generally all forms of
knowledge becoming appreciated in my eyes, that such a profound change could
occur.

Yet my mental health still troubles me, although a lot calmer than before
my life is still hectic in comparison I am a new man, born again - of water and
spirit, word and deed. Transformed inside, and out - from inside to out. I try
to absorb myself each in something whether it a new aspect of a familiar subject
or one brand new. My aim is to never stop learning, exploring, expanding my
horizons with curiosity and heartfelt intrigue. I am if anything a curios soul.

Its so cliche but its true. I feel the moment I stop reaching into the unknown,
the moment I stop trying to solve problems; that's when I will have died, a
spiritual death. I, as I am now, would no longer exist. I would become but a
shell of who I truly am.

I won't make this post too long, so here are some of my interests:
- C and ASM
- Go and Concurrency
- Programming
- Operating Systems and Hardware
- Systems Programming in general
- Writing Runescape Bots
- Philosophy and Theology
- Reading and Studying
- ... (and a lot more to go on)

I am sure I will be writing on some of these subjects soon.
